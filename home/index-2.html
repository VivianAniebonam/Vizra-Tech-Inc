What is eStaff

AI-Powered Gig Platform

For Modern Service Economy

eStaff is an AI-powered gig platform designed to make hiring service providers fast, safe, and seamless. The platform uses intelligent matching, real-time notifications, and identity verification to connect service seekers with qualified workers across Ontario. eStaff is currently in the planning, design, and development phase under Vizra Tech Inc.

Platform Features

Key Features

That Power eStaff

eStaff is built on cutting-edge technology to deliver a seamless, secure, and intelligent gig marketplace experience for all users.

  • Verified Workers - Profiles, background checks, and document tracking that families can trust
  • Reliable Scheduling - Real-time availability, shift acceptance, and automated replacements
  • Attendance & Audits - GPS-verified check-ins, shift histories, and incident reporting for accountability
  • Smart Matching - AI matching that prioritizes skills, availability, language and cultural fit
  • Transparent Pricing & Reputation - Clear fees, ratings, and verified shift history to build trust
  • AI Matching Engine - Connects seekers with the best worker using skills, availability, location, and history
  • Full Verification Workflow - ID, selfie match, police checks, residency proof, certification uploads
  • Real-Time Gig Alerts - Workers receive instant notifications for matching opportunities
  • In-App Communication - Secure chat and optional call features
  • Reputation System - Ratings, reviews, and AI-generated summaries
  • Admin Dashboard - Manage verification, disputes, analytics, and trends
