FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

About Our Services

  • How long does it take to develop a custom application?

    Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. An MVP typically takes 6-12 weeks, while a full-scale application can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

  • What's your pricing model?

    We offer flexible pricing options: fixed-price projects, hourly rates, or dedicated team models. Pricing depends on project scope, technology stack, and timeline. Contact us for a customized quote.

  • What technologies do you work with?

    We specialize in React, Node.js, Python, React Native, AWS, Azure, AI/ML frameworks, and more. We select the best tech stack based on your project requirements and scalability needs.

  • Do you provide post-launch support?

    Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance, bug fixes, feature updates, and 24/7 technical support packages. We ensure your application stays secure, performant, and up-to-date.

  • What industries do you serve?

    We work across diverse industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, SaaS, education, logistics, and more. Our solutions are tailored to meet industry-specific challenges and compliance requirements.

  • How do you handle communication during projects?

    We use Slack, email, and video calls for regular updates. You'll have a dedicated project manager and access to project tracking tools. We follow agile sprints with weekly demos and progress reports.

  • Who owns the code and intellectual property?

    You retain full ownership of all code, designs, and intellectual property we create for your project. We sign NDAs and provide complete source code documentation upon project completion.

  • How do I get started?

    Simply reach out through our contact form, schedule a free consultation, or email us directly. We'll discuss your project, provide recommendations, and create a tailored proposal with timeline and cost estimates.
